Support the Virginia Solar Database


To expand this powerful public resource and ensure it keeps pace with the rapidly changing energy landscape — including the integration of battery storage — we’re seeking to raise $300,000 to support the next three years of work:

  • Continue to provide this as a free, unbiased, and trusted public resource
    • Quarterly updates of project data
    • Presentations and quarterly reports
  • Expand datapoints published
  • Improve geospatial mapping and compatibility with external sources
  • Issue monthly webscraping report (for donors)
  • Expand to include battery storage
    • Convene stakeholder group to define and prioritize storage datapoints
As a thank you for your support, we will offer the following benefits to donors:
  • Your company logo will be displayed on this website and subsequent database reports
  • Advance access to quarterly data updates
  • Monthly copy of Webscraping Report (summary of solar and/or storage on upcoming public notices and hearing agendas)
  • Access to additional datapoints (examples could include lat/longs, application dates, planning commission dates and actions, board/council dates and actions, permitted conditions, prior project details, etc.)
** To provide transparency and retain public trust in this unbiased data source, all donations will be publicly disclosed.

To make your donation, please visit our Give Campus page via web link or QR code below and select Designation “Energy Transition Initiative”
To discuss donating or for major donations larger than $25,000, please contact Elizabeth Marshall at emm2t@virginia.edu.
